Magic and Science: What's the Connection?

“In some ways, card magic and paleontology—two of my specific areas of expertise—seem rather like fundamental opposites of one another. The first is designed to make the ordinary mysterious, while the latter hopes to make the mysterious understandable. Nevertheless, in my mind, both the art of magic and the practice of science have the power to evoke a similar response: wonder. Wonder, I think, is a precious and seriously underrated value. Sometimes it can motivate us to learn more. Sometimes it's just an amazement at how little we know. Regardless of our level of knowledge, wonder at its best is a feeling of pure joy at the intricate and amazing world we're a part of. Our universe is magnificent—I'd even say beautiful. How lucky we are to be conscious (whatever that means) here and now to experience it!” ~ Milo Yeates

Milo, the Magician

With eight years of experience in magic and four years of experience doing professional shows, Milo has been hired to perform a huge variety of shows from intimate backyard parties, to elementary and middle school end-of-year celebrations, to university functions, to large corporate events. With personal mentoring from other professional magicians (including some who have been featured on Penn & Teller Fool Us), and also with experience himself mentoring new magicians at the University of Utah's Magicians Performance Club, Milo has a deep knowledge of the art of magic and excels at everything from close-up sleight of hand card magic to interactive comedy stage magic. Above all else, for Milo, magic is not about the magician performing to the audience—it is about creating a memorable shared experience where everyone has a ton of fun.

Show Options

Here is a collection of the different types of shows Milo offers:

Close-Up Magic
Milo has a 30-minute close-up card magic show great for small events. He is also a fantastic walk-around magician, which is perfect for large events where you’re looking for an entertainer to move between groups of people as they mingle.
Stage Magic
Milo offers both 30 and 45 minute interactive comedy magic stage shows. The former is best for smaller events without access to lapel mics and a sound system, while the latter is best for large events with a sound system. Both are designed to be entertaining both because the magic is inexplicable AND because it is full of ridiculous, silly moments that get the audience involved.
Ted-Style Talk
Milo offers a time-adjustable lecture titled, “Science Works, But It Doesn’t Know Everything (And Why You Shouldn’t Be Afraid)” that weaves together his knowledge of science with his thoughts on the philosophy of science and his deep love for the natural world. It can also (with some advance notice) can be subtly tailored to the purpose of your event.
A Special Mix!
Would you like a stage show AND time for walk-around magic? Would you like a close-up show AND a stage show? Would you like a stage show AND a lecture? Would you like a TED-style lecture that ALSO integrates magic tricks and audience interaction? Need something virtual? Reach out to Milo to discuss different options for your event!
